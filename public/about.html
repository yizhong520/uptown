About Us

Uptown is a housing hunting tool made in mind for college students or short-term renters. Uptown is a message board type web application that allows anyone to post about housing sources with a valid account.

Uptown also has a review section for our customers to enter and share their thoughts for the apartment or house. It is truly the one tool needed for anyone looking for a successful long- or short-term renting experience.
